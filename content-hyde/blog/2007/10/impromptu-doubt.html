--- title: "Impromptu: 怀疑 [Doubt]" created: !!timestamp '2007-10-20 14:05:00' --- {% mark post -%}每当怀疑的当时，怀疑的到底是未来的机率还是自己？ 或许被惊扰的心湖虽然映出混杂的画面，还是能够反映出“自己”吧。 Every time in doubt, am I doubting the probabilities of the future or myself? Although the stirred seas of the heart is only capable to reflect a distorted image, but it’s still capable to reflect one’s true “self”.{%- endmark %}
