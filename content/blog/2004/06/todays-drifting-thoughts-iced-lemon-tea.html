--- title: "Today's Drifting Thoughts: Iced Lemon Tea" created: !!timestamp '2004-06-17 12:40:00' tags: - "thoughts" --- Two years ago, I got addicted to iced lemon tea, and for some reasons I told my friends that it tasted like love, yet I pretty much didn't understand why I said that. Now after having my own experience, joy and sorrow of love (well… one sided, anyway), I think I'm starting to understand the sour yet bitter-sweet sensation. Well… just a thought after something happening, guess I might get another packet of instant iced lemon tea when I go to the hypermart.
