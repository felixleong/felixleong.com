--- title: "Question of the Day: 顾忌 [Scruple]" created: !!timestamp '2007-06-08 15:59:00' --- 什么是幸福？ 什么是自由？ 难道一切顺着自己的意愿是幸福吗？ 难道可以毫无顾忌的去做自己想要做的是自由吗？ What is happiness? What is freedom? Could happiness be said as having everything following one's desires? Could freedom be said as doing whatever we pleased without any reservations?
