--- title: "Distractions" created: !!timestamp '2010-05-25 02:00:00' tags: - "card-toons" - "life" - "productivity" --- Mine came in the form of a Facebook game, Bejeweled Biltz. Knowing Popcap had a long string of addictive casual games, I should have known better. It does not necessarily come in the form of a game (for most I suppose it's managing a farm); it could easily be any activity that contribute extremely little or no value but to show some form of “busy-ness”. Usually it's a bad sign when such distractions happened too often.
