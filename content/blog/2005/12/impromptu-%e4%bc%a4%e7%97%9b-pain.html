--- title: "Impromptu: 伤痛 Pain" created: !!timestamp '2005-12-29 14:53:00' --- 有多少次让自己受不必要的伤痛？才发现好多好多伤口是自创的：都是被自己对别人的看法，角度，观点想法所造成的。 How many times we let ourselves bearing the unnecessary pain? Only to notice that a lot of wounds are self inflicted: All caused by our own perspective towards others.
