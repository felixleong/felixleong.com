No matter how hard I tried, I can't seem to bear the pain deep inside. The tears which has been contained seem to have a leak somewhere that it flows down uncontrollably. … Do I deserve all this pain? Now I really don't know whether they are caused by others or by myself… … I wish I knew the answer…
