--- title: "Letting Go" created: !!timestamp '2011-09-30 16:27:51' tags: - "art" - "life" - "poem" - "relationships" --- How many times does one fall in love to someone that is so beautiful and dear and unable to grasp and hold it close My gaze remain locked at this beautiful thing swimming about gracefully without any concern of the attention it receives I clasps my hands together On one hand, it's as if I'm begging for it to come to me While on the other, letting my internal tensions fade away through my fingertips slowly, gently, with ease… and to let it go… Full size art at DeviantArt, and you can buy it as a print if you like it.
