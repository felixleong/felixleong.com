--- title: "Impromptu: Cheap Words, #1" created: !!timestamp '2006-07-26 02:52:00' --- 听到不一定代表知道，知道不一定代表明白，明白不一定代表懂得，懂得不一定代表行动。 Listening doesn't really mean knowing, Knowing doesn't really mean understanding, Understanding doesn't really mean assimilating, Assimilating doesn't really mean manifestation/action.
