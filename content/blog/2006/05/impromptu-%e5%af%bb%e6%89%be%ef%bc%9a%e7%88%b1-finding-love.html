--- title: "Impromptu: 寻找：爱 (Finding : Love)" created: !!timestamp '2006-05-23 01:38:00' --- 许多时候，都会羡慕身边的欢笑和快乐。也因如此倍感寂寞。 或许，身边的爱与幸福也一样吧！ A lot of times, I felt envious to the fun and laughter around myself. And also because of that I felt even lonelier. Perhaps, that's pretty much the same situation with the love and happiness around me.
